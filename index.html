FAQs

What are Human-type Nano Ceramides and their function?

Ceramides are naturally occurring molecules in your skin that help seal in moisture and strengthen skin's barrier function. Astalift's Human-type Nano Ceramides mimic our skin's natural occurring molecules to fulfill two important functions in boosting skin hydration and locking in moisture to ensure optimal protection against the effects of ageing and environmental damage. The Jelly Aquarysta also has Asenyaku extract to encourage natural Ceramide production to reinforce the skin's defense and visibly improves the skin's texture.

How does the Jelly Aquarysta benefit my skin?

Jelly Aquarysta helps strengthen your skin’s natural defense against environmental factors such as pollution and dryness, while rebalancing your skin’s water and oil levels and enhancing its capacity to absorb and retain moisture. With regular use, improvements in skin elasticity, smoothness, moisturization, as well as minimization of wrinkles can be experienced.

Is Jelly Aquarysta suitable for all skin types?

Jelly Aquarysta is effectively formulated for all skin types as it is function-specific (ie. anti-aging, moisture-enhancing and skin-defense strengthening) instead of gender-specific or age-specific.

Why is Jelly Aquarysta applied onto skin before and not after a toner?

Jelly Aquarysta functions as a leave-on skin conditioner that protects and repairs your skin barrier after cleansing (much like how a conditioner protects your hair after shampooing). Applying Jelly Aquarysta after cleansing before your toner significantly improves the moisture-retention capabilities of the skin, helping beneficial ingredients from products to be absorbed more easily.

What is in Jelly Aquarysta that makes it effective?

Jelly Aquarysta combines the powers of Nano-Astaxanthin, Nano-Lycopene and 3 functional collagens to boost anti-oxidation protection, effectively fighting free-radical damage and delaying skin aging. Added Asenyaku extract and Human-type Nano-Ceramides facilitate optimal absorption of ingredients and boosts moisture levels in skin.
